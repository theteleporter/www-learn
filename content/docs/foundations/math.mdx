# Applied Math - Resource Collection

Math is the language of engineering, providing tools to model, analyze, and solve real-world problems. This page contains a curated collection of resources across essential mathematical areas for engineers.

## Core Mathematical Areas

### Algebra & Equations
Expressing relationships between variables, constraints, and unknowns for system modeling.

**Essential Resources:**
- [Khan Academy – Algebra 1](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/algebra) - Comprehensive beginner course
- [Paul's Online Math Notes](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/) - Detailed tutorials and practice problems
- [Algebra – Brilliant](https://brilliant.org/courses/algebra-foundations/) - Interactive problem-solving approach
- [Purplemath](https://www.purplemath.com/modules/index.htm) - Practical algebra lessons

### Geometry & Trigonometry
Understanding shapes, spatial relationships, and angular measurements essential for engineering applications.

**Key Resources:**
- [Khan Academy – High School Geometry](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/geometry) - Complete geometry course
- [Brilliant – Geometry Fundamentals](https://brilliant.org/courses/geometry-fundamentals/) - Interactive learning
- [3Blue1Brown – Essence of Linear Algebra (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDPD3MizzM2xVFitgF8hE_ab) - Visual explanations
- [GeoGebra](https://www.geogebra.org/geometry) - Interactive geometry tool

### Calculus
Modeling change, motion, and continuous systems through derivatives and integrals.

**Learning Resources:**
- [3Blue1Brown – Essence of Calculus (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WUvTyaaNkzM) - Intuitive visual approach
- [MIT OCW – Calculus 1](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/mathematics/18-01sc-single-variable-calculus-fall-2010/) - University-level lectures
- [Calculus – Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/calculus-1) - Structured course with practice
- [Paul's Online Math Notes – Calculus](https://tutorial.math.lamar.edu/Classes/CalcI/CalcI.aspx) - Detailed explanations

### Probability & Statistics
Reasoning about uncertainty, analyzing data, and making informed decisions.

**Resources:**
- [Khan Academy – Statistics & Probability](https://www.khanacademy.org/math/statistics-probability) - Comprehensive course
- [StatQuest – YouTube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/@statquest) - Clear statistical explanations
- [Probability – Brilliant](https://brilliant.org/courses/probability/) - Interactive probability course
- [Seeing Theory](https://seeing-theory.brown.edu/) - Visual introduction to statistics

### Discrete Mathematics
The mathematical foundation for computer science, including sets, graphs, and combinatorics.

**Key Resources:**
- [Discrete Math by Shai Simonson (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLUl4u3cNGP61MdtwGTqZA0MreSaDybji8) - Lecture series
- [Brilliant – Discrete Mathematics](https://brilliant.org/courses/discrete-mathematics/) - Interactive learning
- [Discrete Mathematics – MIT OpenCourseWare](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/electrical-engineering-and-computer-science/6-042j-mathematics-for-computer-science-fall-2010/) - Rigorous academic content
- [Book of Proof](https://www.people.vcu.edu/~rhammack/BookOfProof/) - Free textbook on mathematical reasoning

## Books & Textbooks

### Foundational Texts
- _"Calculus" by James Stewart_ - Comprehensive calculus textbook
- _"Introduction to Mathematical Thinking" by Keith Devlin_ - Transition to advanced mathematics
- _"Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications" by Kenneth Rosen_ - Standard discrete math reference
- _"The Art of Problem Solving" series_ - Problem-solving focused approach

### Engineering Applications
- _"Advanced Engineering Mathematics" by Erwin Kreyszig_ - Comprehensive engineering math reference
- _"Mathematical Methods for Physicists" by Arfken_ - Applied mathematical methods
